OUR VISION

Delivering wild seafood to the global market, using the best practice and be responsible,
"what is the best for the ocean, is best for us".

OUR STORY

Our company's story began in 1985 when we leased our first bulk cargo ship to provide services to Southeast Asia and China, establishing new trade routes and focusing on foreign imports.

In 2005, we further expanded our business to the American market by establishing a subsidiary called Da Yang Seafood. This expansion included trading and processing of sardines, crabs, shrimp, and Pacific fish.

Then, in 2008, we established the largest tuna production chain in Taiwan at Kaohsiung's Qianzhen Fishing Port. This encompassed the processing, preservation, and logistics of superfrozen tuna, as well as the opening of our first tuna specialty store. With the principles of "direct from the fishing grounds" and "carefully selected ingredients," we provide customers with fresh and distinctive meals.

LOCATIONS

Since the establishment of our first tuna sushi store in 2008, we have expanded to 18 stores across Taiwan, providing our customers with a professional and fresh dining experience. We are committed to continuous innovation and maintaining our passion as we aim to extend our business philosophy globally, allowing more people to experience our services and dedication.
